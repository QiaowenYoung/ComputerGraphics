User-guide and features

The canvas is 600x600 in dimension.

Draw a tapered cylinder on the canvas, with the color red and the light color white. The light comes from [1, 1, 1].

Click on the toggle below canvas to change view from top to side. Click back and change the view back.

The second toggle is to switch between flat shading and wireframe.

The third toggle is to display normals.

Open console to view the list of all the points' coordinates of the tapered cylinder by entering "points".

Open console to view the list of normals by entering "n".

You need to include Matsuda/Lea lib folder under the same directory with prog1 to see trees.
